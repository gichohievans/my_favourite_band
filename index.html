UB40 BIOGRAPHY

The UB40 was an 8 man band formed in 1978. It was originally from Birmingham West Midlands in the United Kingdom.
The name UB40 originated from the british government social welfare form, UB40(Unemployment Benefit form 40).

They are my favourite band because they are able to promote reggae music with clean content for a diverse crowd.
